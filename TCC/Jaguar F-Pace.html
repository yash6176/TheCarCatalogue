JAGUAR F-PACE

Specifications of Jaguar F-Pace

The Jaguar F-Pace is the British manufacturer's first ever SUV.
It is available in a single Prestige variant, which is priced at Rs 60.02 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).

The Jaguar F-PACE has 1 Petrol Engine on offer.
The Petrol engine is 1997 cc. It is available with the Automatic transmission.
Depending upon the variant and fuel type the F-PACE has a mileage of 14.38 kmpl.

The F-PACE is a 5 seater SUV and has a length of 4731mm, width of 2175mm and a wheelbase of 2874mm.


Notable features in the Jaguar F-Pace include connected-car tech, Android Auto and quad-zone climate control, wireless phone charger,
drive select, ambient lighting, park assist, wireless phone charger as well as keyless entry with push button start.
Safety features in the Jaguar f-Pace include eight airbags, ABS with EBD, front and rear parking sensors, rear parking camera,
electronic stability program, traction control, electric parking with auto hold and lane departure warning.
