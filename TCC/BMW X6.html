BMW X6

Specifications of BMW X6

BMW X6 Latest Updates The 2020 X6 is all new and represents the model's third generation. Along with shorter and wider dimensions than its predecessor, BMW also stretched the wheelbase and overall length. The new X6 is now also more chiseled on the outside and offers an illuminated grille for the first time.

Inside, the styling is virtually identical to all of the company's latest models. A pair of 12.3-inch screens are standard along with many other available luxuries. The 2020 X6 has updated turbocharged six-cylinder and twin-turbo V-8 powertrains, too.

BMW will offer the 2020 generation X6 with four engine options. The petrol motors include a 340bhp 3.0-litre, six cylinder unit and a 530bhp 4.4-litre V8 unit. The 3.0-litre, six cylinder unit will be available in two states of tune, 400bhp and 265bhp. An eight-speed automatic transmission will be standard across the range. The third generation BMW X6 is expected to be launched in India in 2020. BMW X6 price starts at ₹ 95.00 Lakh and goes upto ₹ 95.00 Lakh. The price of Petrol version for X6 is ₹ 95.00 Lakh.
