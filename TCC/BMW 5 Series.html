BMW 5 Series

Specifications of BMW 5 Series

BMW launched the all-new 5 Series in India at a starting price of Rs 49.9 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). It is available in four trim levels 520d SportLine, 520d LuxuryLine, 530i SportLine and 530d M Sport. Prices for these variants are Rs 49.9 lakh, Rs 53.6 lakh, Rs 49.9 lakh and Rs 61.3 lakh respectively.

Courtesy of the new platform, which has increased the use of aluminium and high-strength steel, the new 5 Series is lighter by up to 70kg as opposed to the previous model. Further, it also borrows its design cues and features from BMW's flagship sedan, the 7 Series.
The India-spec 2017 5 Series is offered with three engine options: 520d (2.0-litre 4-cylinder diesel, 190PS/400Nm), 530d (3.0-litre 6-cylinder diesel, 265PS/620Nm) and 530i (2.0-litre four-cylinder petrol, 252PS/350Nm). All the three engines come mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The 5 Series will go up against the likes of the Mercedes-Benz E-Class, Audi A6, Volvo S90 and the Jaguar XF.
